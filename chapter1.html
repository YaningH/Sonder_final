87.6 FM EXISTENCE

We see their faces but not their stories. We notice their presence but not their struggles.

“We are each the center of our own universe.”

hardest realization is accepting that other people are just as real as we are, not as tools, examples, or obstacles, but as individuals with expectations, emotions, and inner lives as vivid as our own. And yet, the moment we leave a room, that space vanishes from our conscious experience, ceasing to exist in our world, while for others, it remains alive, unfolding, and entirely separate from us.
