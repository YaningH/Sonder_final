106.5 FM OBSCURE SORROWS

Sonder is one of the many "obscure sorrows"

Koenig describes—a sadness that comes from the realization that everyone has their own full, rich life that may never intersect with yours.

This sorrow isn’t one of despair but of quiet, reflective understanding. It’s the feeling that there are infinite lives, all equally complex, and we can only catch glimpses of them. Each person’s story remains mostly hidden, and we are left with a sense of longing to know more.
